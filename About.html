About Us

Fresh Fruit Producers Group Ltd. was established in 2012 and brings together 28 fruit producers . Our orchard area is over 300 ha and the annual fruit harvest is over 25 000 tonnes. The company’s head office is located in Mszczonów, while the production plant is situated in Kowiesy, in the vicinity of the S8 route, close to the A2 Wiskitki motorway junction, and the transit route No. 50 connecting the east and west of the country. Thanks to such a carefully chosen location, we are the best logistically situated group in the country.
